# ⚙️ 1. Power (^) என்றால் என்ன?

👉 definition:

```text
2^n = 2 × 2 × 2 × ... (n times)
```

---

## Examples

```text
2^3 = 2 × 2 × 2 = 8
2^2 = 2 × 2 = 4
2^1 = 2
```
